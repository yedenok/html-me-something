☆Backstory☆

This is the world of Lakore. Lakore has modern level sensibilities, and modern level technology... in the north. In the south part of the world grows an entity known as The Chaos. It's a massive, intangible force of entropy. The closer one gets to the Chaos, the less technology works. It can manipulate probability to make things fail in ways they otherwise wouldn't, like exploding. No one knows if the Chaos is sentient or simply a force of nature (truthfully, few even know about it in the first place), but it's clear that if it keeps growing, the whole world will eventually be sent back to medieval times.

The world of Lakore also has a single goddess. Known only as the Glinting Lady, she has a small following in the forest of [Name]. Over a decade ago, the Glinting Lady took part of her own magic, and sent it out into the world to cause the birth of six Heroes who would one day fight the Chaos. That day is today...

☆Our Heroes☆

☆Heather Goodwin☆

Heather is a 17 year old girl living in the [Name] Forest commune. Both of her parents were followers of the Glinting Lady, and Heather was raised primarily by the goddess herself. She is the only character available at the start of the game, being sent out to find the other heroes. Heather is a Support class, and specializes in magic that heals and fortifies her teammates.

☆Dedrick Maynard☆

Dedrick is a 16 year old boy living in the town of [Name]. He's a kind individual who spends his time watching the town's children. He joins the party when Heather tells him that the Chaos poses a threat to his neighbors. Dedrick is a Defense class, with lots of health and toughness, and fights with a sword.

☆Li Yanmei☆

Yanmei is a 16 year old girl living in the city of [Name]. She's gorgeous and charismatic from a wealthy family, and people she meets can't help but to do what she wants. She joins the party to get away from her parents. Yanmei is an Offense class, and specializes in damaging magic like fireballs.

☆Basir Hakim☆

Basir is a 10 year old boy living in the city of [Name]. He attends [Name] Academy, a prestigious institute for geniuses. He's fairly shy and humble despite his massive intellect, and often hides behind his adoptive older sister, Zakiya. He joins the party after Zakiya promises to stay with him. Basir is a Support class, and specializes in magic that weakens and immobilizes his foes.

☆Carlyn O'Kelley☆

Carlyn is a 10 year old girl living in a forest with her brother Sean and their friend Niyol. Her brother Sean took her and ran away into the forest 5 years ago, where they have been hunting and gathering ever since. She joins the party after they agree to allow Sean to join as well. Carlyn is an Offense class with super strength, and she fights with a giant hammer.

☆Niyol Fletcher☆

Niyol is 15 years old and genderfluid. They are fast and dexterous. Their superhuman abilities led to them being kept in a lab for much of their young life, but they eventually escaped and fled to the forest where Carlyn and Sean were living. Niyol is a Defense class, specializing in dodging attacks, and dual wields knives.

☆Zakiya Travere☆

Zakiya is a 16 year old girl living in the city of [Name]. She attends [Name] Academy with Basir, and acts as his older sister. She is not one of the goddess's heroes, but joins the party anyway because Basir is too scared to go alone. Even though she doesn't have magic, she's still formidable. Zakiya is a Special class, with offensive and supportive abilites using technology that she can make work even inside the Chaos.

☆Sean O'Kelley☆

Sean is a 17 year old boy, and Carlyn's brother. He is not one of the heroes, but joins the party because Carlyn refuses to go without him. 5 years ago, he heard a story about a little kid being taken away to a lab to study their superhuman abilities, and, fearing the same would happen to his super strong sister, ran away with her. He is a Special class, fighting with a bow, as well as being able to disarm traps and pick locks.
